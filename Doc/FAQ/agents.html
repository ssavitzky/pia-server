What does agent _____ do?

Agency
main agent-- manages all other agents
History
maintains list of visited web pages -- see below
fileTools
adds control toolbar to local files served by your Information Agency
remoteTools
adds control toolbar to pages proxied through your agency
Demo
demonstrates features of the PIA's markup language (the extension of HTML in which agents are written)
PIA
serves files located in the PIA_HOME directory
ROOT
handles documents with URL's that do not refer to any known agent.
